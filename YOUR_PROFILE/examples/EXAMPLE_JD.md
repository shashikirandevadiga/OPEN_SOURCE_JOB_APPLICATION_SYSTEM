## Product Manager - Data Platform

**Company**: TechCorp Inc.
**Location**: San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
**Type**: Full-time

### About the Role

We're looking for a Product Manager to lead our data platform initiatives. You'll work with engineering, data science, and business teams to build products that help our customers make better decisions with their data.

### Responsibilities

- Define and own the product roadmap for our data platform
- Work closely with engineering to prioritize features and manage the backlog
- Conduct user research and translate insights into product requirements
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams including sales, marketing, and customer success
- Define and track key metrics to measure product success
- Present product strategy to leadership and stakeholders

### Requirements

**Required**:
- 5+ years of product management experience
- Experience with data products or platforms
- Strong SQL skills and familiarity with data tools (Tableau, Looker, etc.)
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
- Track record of shipping products from 0-to-1
- Experience with Agile/Scrum methodologies

**Preferred**:
- Technical background (CS degree or equivalent experience)
- Experience at a B2B SaaS company
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Experience with machine learning or AI products

### What We Offer

- Competitive salary and equity
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Flexible PTO
- Remote-friendly culture
- Professional development budget
